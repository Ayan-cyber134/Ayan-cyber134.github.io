Zia Bot: Rules and Punishments

All users interacting with Zia Bot are expected to follow these rules. Violations will result in severe consequences, up to permanent service termination and legal action.

1. Impersonation of Zia Bot Team

Rule: Users must not impersonate Zia Bot team members or staff. This includes usernames, avatars, or any false claims of affiliation.

Punishment:

  • 1st Offense: Warning.
  • 2nd Offense: 14-day server ban.
  • 3rd Offense: Permanent ban and potential service-wide restrictions.

2. Unauthorized Use or Redistribution of Zia Bot Code/Assets

Rule: Users must not copy, distribute, reverse-engineer, or monetize Zia Bot’s code, assets, or functionality without permission.

Punishment:

  • 1st Offense: Immediate 30-day service-wide suspension.
  • 2nd Offense: Permanent service termination and legal action.

3. Attacking, Exploiting, or Hacking Zia Bot

Rule: Any attempt to disrupt, exploit, or gain unauthorized access to Zia Bot will result in severe consequences.

Punishment:

  • 1st Offense: Immediate permanent service-wide termination.
  • Severe cases: Legal action and reporting to Discord Trust & Safety.

4. Misuse of Commands

Rule: Spamming, exploiting, or misusing commands for harassment or disruption is prohibited.

Punishment:

  • 1st Offense: Warning.
  • 2nd Offense: 7-day restriction from Zia Bot.
  • 3rd Offense: Permanent restriction and potential service suspension.

5. Circumventing Punishments

Rule: Bypassing punishments by using alternate accounts or other methods is strictly forbidden.

Punishment:

  • 1st Offense: All accounts suspended for 30 days.
  • 2nd Offense: Permanent termination from Zia Bot services on all accounts.

6. False Reporting

Rule: Submitting false or misleading reports against users or Zia Bot is prohibited.

Punishment:

  • 1st Offense: Warning.
  • 2nd Offense: 30-day restriction from Zia Bot.
  • 3rd Offense: Permanent service-wide termination.

7. Exploiting Bugs or Vulnerabilities

Rule: Users must report bugs or vulnerabilities. Exploiting them for any reason is forbidden.

Punishment:

  • 1st Offense: Immediate 60-day suspension.
  • 2nd Offense: Permanent service termination and possible legal action.

8. Unauthorized Data Access or Sharing

Rule: Accessing, sharing, or manipulating Zia Bot’s logs or data without authorization is prohibited.

Punishment:

  • 1st Offense: 90-day suspension.
  • 2nd Offense: Permanent termination and legal action.

9. Promoting or Supporting Malicious Activities

Rule: Users must not encourage or support actions harmful to Zia Bot or its users, such as promoting attacks or sharing exploits.

Punishment:

  • Immediate permanent service-wide termination and report to Discord Trust & Safety.

10. Threats or Harassment Against Zia Bot or Team

Rule: Threatening or harassing the Zia Bot team or its functionality will not be tolerated.

Punishment:

  • Immediate permanent service termination and potential legal action.

11. Violations of Discord’s Community Guidelines

Rule: Any violation of Discord’s Terms of Service or Community Guidelines while using Zia Bot will result in strict enforcement.

Punishment:

  • Immediate escalation to Discord Trust & Safety.
  • Permanent service termination for severe cases.

Note: Zia Bot reserves the right to enforce punishments based on the severity of violations. Severe cases will involve Discord Trust & Safety and legal authorities.
